The Injury Tracker — Support

Getting Started

  • Tap Add Injury to create a new injury log.
  • Enter details: injury type, date, severity, and notes.
  • Save your entry.

Viewing Injuries

  • Go to the Injuries tab to see all logged injuries.
  • Tap an injury to see full details.

Adding Notes

  • Open an injury entry and tap Add Note.
  • Record symptoms, treatments, or progress updates.

Editing & Deleting

  • Tap Edit on any injury or note to change info.
  • Use Delete to remove entries permanently.

Sharing Data

  • Use the Share button to export injury logs via email or other apps.

FAQs

  • Can I track more than one injury? — Yes, multiple injuries are supported.
  • Is my data secure? — Yes, your data is stored securely on your device.
  • What if I lose my phone? — Back up your data regularly if the app supports it.
